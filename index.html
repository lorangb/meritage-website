FAQS

Frequently Asked Questions

Where are you located?

Meritage is headquartered in Lyons, Colorado, with team members across the United States. We work with companies nationwide, both in-person and virtually.

Who is behind Meritage?

Meritage was founded by a group of experienced operators, entrepreneurs, and investors who came together around a shared insight: the companies that win are not always the ones with the best ideas or the most capital — they are the ones that execute with discipline while others chase shiny objects.

Meritage exists to be the partner we wish we had: grounded, practical, and deeply experienced in execution, capital, and modernization.

What is a Business Operating System?

A Business Operating System is a set of tools, processes, and disciplines that help a leadership team run their company with clarity, alignment, and accountability. It turns the art of management into a repeatable system.

What is EOS® (Entrepreneurial Operating System)?

EOS® is a complete business operating system designed to simplify and streamline even the most complex business challenges. It focuses on six key components of a healthy business: Vision, People, Data, Issues, Process, and Traction.

Why did we choose EOS® as our preferred Business Operating System?

We chose EOS® because it is practical, comprehensive, and proven across thousands of companies. It provides a common language and framework that works whether you are a 10-person startup or a 1,000-person enterprise.

What kind of companies does Meritage work with?

We work with companies of all sizes across industries. What matters most is not size or industry, but mindset: leaders who are open to change, committed to growth, and more concerned about standing still than doing the hard work required to move forward.
